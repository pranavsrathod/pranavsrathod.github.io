<h2>Introduction to Machine Learning</h2>
<p class="lead">Machine learning is a field of artificial intelligence that uses statistical techniques to give computer systems the ability to "learn" from data, without being explicitly programmed.</p>
<p>In this blog post, we will explore the basics of machine learning, its applications, and how it is transforming various industries.</p>

<h3>What is Machine Learning?</h3>
<p>Machine learning involves the development of algorithms that can process large amounts of data to identify patterns and make predictions.</p>
<img src="../images/machine-learning.png" alt="Machine Learning" class="img-fluid rounded" />
<p>There are different types of machine learning, including supervised learning, unsupervised learning, and reinforcement learning.</p>

<h3>Applications of Machine Learning</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Healthcare: Predictive analytics and personalized treatment plans.</li>
  <li>Finance: Fraud detection and algorithmic trading.</li>
  <li>Retail: Recommendation systems and customer segmentation.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Conclusion</h3>
<p>Machine learning is revolutionizing the way we interact with technology. As data continues to grow, the importance of machine learning will only increase, making it a critical area of study and application in the future.</p>
